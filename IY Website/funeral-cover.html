Funeral Cover

Affordable peace of mind for you and your family

Funeral cover that takes the financial pressure off your loved ones at the moment they need it least.

Why funeral cover matters

Losing a loved one is hard enough without the added stress of covering funeral costs on short notice. Funeral cover gives your family immediate access to funds so they can focus on saying goodbye, not on finances.

What our funeral cover includes

  • Affordable monthly premiums, tailored to your household budget.
  • Options to cover yourself, your spouse, children and extended family members.
  • A straightforward claims process designed to pay out quickly when your family needs it most.

Peace of mind, built in

We'll walk you through the cover levels available and help you choose a plan that fits your family's needs and your monthly budget — with no confusing fine print.
