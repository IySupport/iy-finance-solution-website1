Credit Clearance

Improve your credit profile with expert help

From credit assessments to account negotiations and judgement removals, we help clear the way to a healthier credit record.

What credit clearance covers

A damaged credit record can follow you for years, affecting everything from loan applications to job offers. Our credit clearance service works across several fronts to help restore your profile.

Services included

  • Credit assessment — a full review of your credit bureau profile to identify what's affecting your score.
  • Account negotiations — direct engagement with credit providers on your behalf to settle or restructure outstanding accounts.
  • Judgement removal — assistance with rescinding or removing court judgements where legally possible.
  • Default & administration order review — checking your record for defaults or administration orders that can be addressed or corrected.

Why work with us

Our advisers have inside experience from South Africa's major banks, giving us a practical understanding of how credit providers assess and report on your account — and how to negotiate accordingly.
