Financial Assessment

Personalised guidance for informed financial decisions

A clear-eyed look at your income, debt and goals — so every decision you make afterward is an informed one.

What a financial assessment involves

Before recommending any solution — whether that's debt counselling, a loan, or simply a budgeting adjustment — we start with an honest assessment of where you actually stand financially.

What we look at

  • Your monthly income against your essential expenses.
  • All outstanding debt and its terms, interest rates and repayment history.
  • Your credit bureau profile and what it means for your future borrowing.
  • Your short and long-term financial goals.

The outcome

You'll leave the assessment with a clear, written picture of your finances and a recommended next step — whether that's one of our services or simply a few practical changes you can make on your own.
