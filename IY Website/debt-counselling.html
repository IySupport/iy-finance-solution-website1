Debt Counselling

Professional debt review that protects what you've worked for

A structured, legally regulated route out of over-indebtedness — without losing your home, your car, or your peace of mind.

What is debt counselling?

Debt counselling, also known as debt review, is a formal process under the National Credit Act that helps over-indebted consumers restructure their debt into a single, affordable monthly instalment — while protecting their assets from repossession.

As a registered credit provider, we work directly with your credit providers to negotiate lower instalments over a longer term, so your monthly budget actually works for your life.

How the process works

  • Step 1 — Assessment. A registered debt counsellor reviews your income, expenses and debt to confirm whether you qualify as over-indebted.
  • Step 2 — Negotiation. We approach your credit providers to restructure your repayments into terms you can actually afford each month.
  • Step 3 — Court confirmation. The new repayment plan is confirmed by the courts, giving you legal protection from creditors while you repay.

Who this is for

If you're struggling to keep up with multiple accounts, receiving constant calls from creditors, or worried about losing your car or home, debt counselling may be the safest way to regain control — regardless of how much you owe or whether you have assets.
