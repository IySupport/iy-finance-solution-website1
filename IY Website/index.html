How It Works

Your path to financial freedom, in four steps

Debt counselling in South Africa follows a legally regulated process. Here's exactly what to expect once you reach out to us.

01

Free assessment

We review your income, debt and expenses to determine whether you're over-indebted and which solution fits your situation.

02

Debt review

If you qualify, a registered debt counsellor negotiates with your credit providers to restructure your repayments into one affordable instalment.

03

Court confirmation

The new repayment plan is taken to court, which confirms the order and protects you from creditors while you repay under the new terms.

04

Ongoing support

We stay with you for the full journey — tracking payments, answering questions and helping you plan for the day you're declared debt-free.
